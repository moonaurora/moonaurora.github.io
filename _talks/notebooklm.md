This talk shares a practical workflow for using NotebookLM to assist academic PPT creation. It covers NotebookLM’s source-grounded question answering and presentation generation capabilities, and discusses a post-processing pipeline for converting generated slides into editable presentation files.

The talk also compares NotebookLM with other tools such as Gemini and Canva, highlighting the trade-off between creativity and editability in AI-assisted slide production. In addition, it discusses the current limitations of these tools, including OCR errors, formatting issues, and the need for manual refinement in real-world academic presentation preparation.
